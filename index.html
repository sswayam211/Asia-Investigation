About Us

Our longstanding history of offering insurance carriers top-notch investigative services is one of the pillars of Asia Investigation Services . From coast to coast, our investigation services—which include background checks, activity checks, and casualty surveillance—have assisted hundreds of defense lawyers and adjusters in obtaining the data they require to make informed, responsible claims decisions.
We understand that too many insurance investigation firms make excessively bold claims about what they can do, which nearly always results in you appearing unfavorable to your managers, the claims team, and the claimant. We are aware that choosing an investigator can be difficult and that most insurance investigators will sound the same when they offer their services.
